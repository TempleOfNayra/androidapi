Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone Settings:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Nayra
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've previously purchased a subscription, you can restore it by:

  1. Opening the Nayra app
  2. Going to Settings
  3. Tapping "Restore Purchases"

Why am I having trouble signing in?

Make sure you're using the same sign-in method (Apple or Google) that you used when creating your account. If you're still having issues, please contact us.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at support@nayra.app with your request.
